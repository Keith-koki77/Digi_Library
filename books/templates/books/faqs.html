Frequently Asked Questions

How can I create an account?

To create an account, simply click on the "Sign Up" button on the top navigation bar. Fill in the required information and follow the prompts to complete the registration process.

How do I search for books?

You can search for books by clicking the Browse Button located at the top of the page in the Dropdown Menu Button. You can also browse through different categories to discover books.

Can I download books for offline reading

Yes, if the book is available for download. You can check the book details to see if it's available for download and follow the instructions provided to download the book.
